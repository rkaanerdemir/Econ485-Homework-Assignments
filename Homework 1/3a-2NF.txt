Relationships:
​One COURSE has many SECTIONs (1‑N).
One INSTRUCTOR teaches many SECTIONs (1‑N).
One STUDENT can register to many SECTIONs, and one SECTION can have many STUDENTs, so STUDENT and SECTION are N‑M through REGISTRATION.

ER diagram (logical description):
STUDENT (1) — (N) REGISTRATION (N) — (1) SECTION
COURSE (1) — (N) SECTION
INSTRUCTOR (1) — (N) SECTION

The design has no repeating groups and no attribute depending on part of a composite key, so it is in 2NF.
